Quick Summary:

1. Introduction

Daily Scanner is a privacy-first document scanning app. All scanning, processing, and storage happen entirely on your device.

Automatically (via AdMob):

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Information

App Features:

We DO NOT:

4. Permissions

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage.
